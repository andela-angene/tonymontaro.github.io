Privacy Policy

When you provide information through AllKidsFair.com (our Site), your privacy is respected. All visits to our Site and all information provided or collected is subject to this Privacy Policy.

1. Personally Identifiable Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information about you, unless you specifically provide it to us. If you complete a user registration form, online survey or purchase, you may be asked to provide certain information such as your contact information, delivery and billing addresses, credit card number, etc. If you prefer that we not collect any personally identifiable information from you, please do not provide us with the information. When you do submit personally identifiable information on our Site, you consent to the collection, use and disclosure of the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about upcoming events, specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

a. Opt-In Email

If you opt-in during the registration process or at any other time, the information you provide may be used by us to send you email messages, including newsletters, surveys or product and event information. If you prefer not to receive these emails, please do not opt-in to receive them. If you initially opt-in and later decide that you would no longer like to receive these emails, you may opt-out (see Section 8).

b. Service Email

If you provide us with your contact information, we may send you service-related email concerning our Site, customer service requests or orders. These service email are necessary to serve you, respond to your concerns and provide a high level of customer service.

c. Non-Disclosure to Third Parties

We will not provide your personally identifiable information to non-affiliated third parties for their use in marketing their products or services to you.

d. Fraud Protection and Legal Compliance

We may disclose any information provided by you, if we deem disclosure necessary to comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request or to prevent credit or other fraud.

e. Service Providers

We retain other companies and individuals to perform certain functions on our behalf, such as customer support, webhosting, data analysis and messaging. These third parties may be provided with your personally identifiable information in order to perform their functions, but they may not use the information for any other purpose.

2. Internal Use of Information

We may collect, store and use any information you provide through our Site for internal business purposes, such as generating statistics and developing marketing plans.

3. Children under 13

If you are under 13 years of age, you may browse our Site. However, you should not provide personal information to us. Our Site is not designed for children, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

4. Links

Our Site may contain links to or from other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any other websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to the information we collect on our Site. When you link to another websites from our Site, this Privacy Policy does not apply.

5. Security

We use security measures to protect the security of your personal information. Our site is a certified SSL Secured Store.

6. Notification of Changes

We may revise this Privacy Policy. If we do so, we will post the revised policy here. Our rights and responsibilities with regard to information you provide will be based on the Privacy Policy in effect at the time the information is used.

7. Updating Your Information

You can review, change or correct the personally identifiable information you provide by emailing us or, if you are a registered user, by logging in and accessing your account information.

8. Opt-Out

You may opt-out of receiving Opt-In emails by changing your account preferences, by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any Opt-In email you receive from us or by contacting us. The Opt-Out option does not apply to service communications.

9. Comments

We hope your visit to our Site is an excellent one. If you have any questions, comments or concerns about our Site’s Privacy Policy, or anything else, please contact us.
