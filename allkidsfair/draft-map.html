Draft Map

This is the Draft Map for the April 30, 2017 All Kids Fair. Please use this as a guide in helping you select the type of booth that would be best for you.

Note that if rear pipe and drape is not purchased/included, you may not hang a sign or banner at the rear of the booth.


Violet Space (V) is a trapezoid (7' back, 14 foot side, 16' front) Gateway space with 2 tables. $1100. Optional 8' tall pipe and drape (call for details)

Peach Spaces (P) are 10 wide x 8 deep Gateway spaces with 2 tables in an L. $900. Optional 8' tall rear and side pipe and drape.

Orange Spaces (O) are 6 wide x 8 deep Gateway spaces with 1 table. $600. Optional 8' tall rear and side pipe and drape.

Gray Spaces (G) are 6 wide x 6 deep Gateway spaces with 1 table. $450. Optional 8' tall rear and side pipe and drape. (Side is not recommended as there is no space between tables.)

Turquoise spaces (T) are 10 x 10 Grand Ballroom perimeter spaces with 2 tables in an L. $900. Optional 8' tall rear and side pipe and drape.

Denim spaces (D) are 6 x 6 Grand Ballroom perimeter spaces with 1 table. $600. Optional 8' tall rear and side pipe and drape.

Emerald spaces (E) are corner 6 x 6 Grand Ballroom spaces with 1 table $600. 8' rear pipe and drape included.. Optional side pipe and drape.

Blue spaces (B) are 6 x 6 Grand Ballroom spaces with 1 table $500. 8' rear pipe and drape included. Optional side pipe and drape.


We also have the option to purchase a half table space for $300. The location will be determined by the All Kids Fair.

All of our booths come with tables, tablecloths, 2 chairs, and name tags.

Please note that we do not assign spaces until all spots are sold to increase the distance between similar exhibitors and to accommodate the vendors who purchase pipe and drape. We only allow a certain amount of any type of business or organization.

We hope this is helpful. Please let us know what questions we can answer for you.
