Here's what we do

From Heat Treatment of Bed Bugs, W.D.O ( Wood destroying organisms) or V.O.C (Volatile Organic Compounds). Heat Solutions has youcovered and is always on time and on budget.

Bed Bug Elimination
 

Heat is an excellent method to treat for bed bugs as it is one of the few treatments that can eliminate manageable infestations in one treatment by utilizing both direct fire propane and Temp Air heaters, while reducing the amount of pesticides introduced into residential or commercial structures..

Wood Destroying Organisms
(W.D.O)

Heat is a nonchemical option for both full and localized W.D.O treatments, useful for instances where fumigation is not the viable option commonly associated with treatment of multi family structures.

This process involves the heating of all wood in a structure without using chemicals to effectively eradicate manageable infestations in relatively shorter periods of time.

Volatile Organic Compound Treatment (V.O.C)

Volatile organic compounds (VOC) means any compound of carbon excluding carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide,carbonic acid, metallic carbides or carbonates, and ammonium carbonate, which participates in atmospheric photochemical reactions.

Heat is a viable option to reduce current V.O.C Levels by achieving regeneration through heat. Therefore, bringing the V.O.C. to a permittable level.
