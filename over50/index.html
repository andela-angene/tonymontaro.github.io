SAVE THE DATE FOR OUR 2017 EVENT AT ITS NEW, EASIER LOCATION

Our 8th annual Over 50 Fair will be on Sunday, September 17, 2017 from 10-4:30 at the HUNTINGTON HILTON.

The Over 50 Fair was created to celebrate life over age 50. As people are retiring later and living longer, there are more years to have fun and live life to its fullest. Our event is part health and wellness fair, part conference, and part baby-boomer/senior fair. Loads of people came for a great day…and some brought their parents!
